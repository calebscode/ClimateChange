One might expect that with a greater investment into renewable sources of energy, CO2 consumption would be mitigated. In reality, we found little correlation between these two factors. To illustrate this surprising find, we can see that in years with little investment, similar changes were observed in CO2 consumption when compared to years with large investment. Possible factors that may counteract the expected trend may include global access to electricity or population growth.
